Boating in the Backwaters

Experience the tranquility of Kasaragod's backwaters with a peaceful boat ride. The serene environment, surrounded by lush greenery, offers a perfect escape. Houseboat cruises are a popular way to explore the intricate network of canals and lakes.

Fishing Adventures

For those who enjoy angling, Kasaragod's waters are teeming with life. You can try your hand at traditional fishing methods or simply relax by the water with a fishing rod. It's a great way to connect with nature and experience the local way of life.
