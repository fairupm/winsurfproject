About Kasaragod

Kasaragod, located in the northernmost district of Kerala, is a beautiful town known for its rich history, diverse culture, and stunning natural beauty. It's a place where you can find tranquil backwaters, rolling hills, and coconut groves.

Pristine Beaches

Bekal Beach

Bekal Beach is famous for its stunning views of the historic Bekal Fort. The beach is clean, well-maintained, and perfect for a relaxing evening.

Kappil Beach

Kappil Beach is a serene and less-crowded beach, offering a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The Kodi Cliff nearby offers a panoramic view of the Arabian Sea.

Valiyaparamba Backwaters

A stunningly beautiful backwater stretch, Valiyaparamba is fed by four rivers and dotted with numerous little islands. It is a major fishing centre and a delightful getaway.

Tourism

Kasaragod is a paradise for tourists. From the majestic Bekal Fort to the serene backwaters of Valiyaparamba, there is something for everyone. The district is also known for its Theyyam performances, a vibrant and colorful ritual dance.

Entertainment

Kasaragod is blessed with lush green landscapes, from the rolling hills of Ranipuram to the dense forests and plantations. Enjoy trekking, nature walks, and the serene beauty of its verdant scenery.
